If you'd like to learn more about me, take a look at some of my recent projects below.

Codex

Codex is a text editor that is designed from the ground up for people who want to get things done. It marries powerful and convenient features like an embedded terminal, embedded file browser, and support for multiple documents through tabs with a simple, distraction-free user interface.

Tracket

Tracket is a comprehensive anti-theft suite for Windows that allows the user to always stay in control of their computer, no matter who has it. Through the modern, web-based interface, the user can remotely take pictures with their webcam, view what is being typed on their computer, encrypt/decrypt/view their filesystem, and have their computer speak out text.

Nutshell News

Nutshell News is designed for people who move. It aggregates news from a variety of sources and presents the top headlines in a convenient, card-based interface that allows the user to quickly scan the news and follow what matters to them.
